Pothole Assistance


In the event of an incident, the cost to replace your client’s tyre and or rim will be covered. When an incident occurs, if there your client doesn’t have a spare wheel available, their vehicle will be uplifted. If 2 or more wheels were damaged, they will be taken to the nearest repair centre.

CAN’s 'Take Me Home' Service


We offer peer-to-peer ride-sharing services to your clientele – similar to that of Uber, but much more affordable.

Clients Up to 3 additional passengers to one drop-off address.

Service hours for taking bookings are open 24 hours, every day of the week.

Geyser and Burst Pipe Assist


Your clients will be covered on any pipe per incident, including accommodation for electrical failure, burst geyser pipes, valves and more. We also include a call out & labour cost.

The service provider’s account is settled on behalf of your client.

With every incident, your client further receives a 6 months workmanship guarantee on work done on the premises.

Minor Collision and Dent Repair


With each incident your client is covered where the damages/losses fall within their excess amount, as indicated by their insurer, per incident.

We can repair your client’s vehicle at their home or office depending on the type of damage, or the vehicle can even be collected and returned after the repair service.

Your client simply has to take a photo of the affected area, send it through to their allocated CAN adviser and a quote will be finalised shortly.

Battery and Tyre Loyalty Program

Tyres and batteries are supplied by Goodyear through any of their Trentyre branches nationwide.
